Places Must Visit

Lotus Temple

I visited this place in July 2023

The Lotus Temple (also known as Kamal Mandir) in Delhi is a matchless architectural marvel and one of the prime tourist attractions of the National Capital. Shaped in the form of a spectacular lotus with white petals, it makes for a break-taking sight and attracts countless visitors throughout the year. Unlike most other places of worship, this temple or Bahai House of Worship does not allow ritualistic ceremonies and has no fixed pattern to conduct worship.


Los Angles

I visited this place in September 2023

Los Angeles is easily one of the most famous cities in the world! Known for being the home of American cinema, a place for dreams and dreamers, and a backdrop for countless famous TV shows, it’s no wonder so many people are in awe of Los Angeles and all that it has given the world!

The Lion King
nice for one time

I took my kids to watch the movie and they loved it. The story shows how a young lion prince, after the murder of his father, flees his kingdom only to learn the true meaning of responsibility and bravery. This movie is worth a watch for its gorgeous visuals and cinematography. The music was phenomenal. Beyoncé really brought Nala to live but the show stealers were Timon and Pumba. Kids would love it.
